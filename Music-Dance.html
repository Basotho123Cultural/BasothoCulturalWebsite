The Rhythm of Basotho Culture

Music and dance are at the heart of Basotho celebrations, ceremonies, and storytelling. Experience the rhythm and movement that unite communities across Lesotho.

Mokhibo

Performed by women, Mokhibo is a graceful kneeling dance accompanied by clapping and singing, showcasing strength, rhythm, and pride.

Lesiba (Musical Instrument)

The Lesiba is a traditional mouth-resonated instrument made from a string and feather, used to imitate bird sounds and communicate across distances.

Setapa Dance

Traditional step dance emphasizing fast footwork and precise movements.

Indlamu Dance

A warrior-style dance featuring high kicks and rhythmic stomping, showcasing strength and agility.
